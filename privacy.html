Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how WHG Auto Uploader ("the Service") handles data when automating uploads to platforms such as TikTok and YouTube. The Service is used exclusively by its creator and is not available to the public.

Data the Service Processes

The Service processes the minimum necessary data required to upload content. This may include:

  • Video files selected by the creator
  • Metadata such as titles, descriptions, and tags
  • API authentication tokens required by TikTok, YouTube, and other platforms

The Service does not collect, store, or share personal information belonging to external users.

Authentication Data

Authentication with TikTok, YouTube, and other platforms uses official OAuth methods. Access tokens are handled securely by the automation platform (e.g., Make.com) and are not stored or shared elsewhere.

No Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to the Service or any data processed by it. All content and credentials belong solely to the creator.

Use of External APIs

The Service sends video files and metadata to third-party APIs strictly for the purpose of uploading content. These APIs may process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal
  • YouTube: https://policies.google.com/
